Welcome to NextDoor Circle, where neighbors become friends and community thrives! We believe in the power of connection, and our mission is to bring together the amazing people in our neighborhood. Whether you're looking to join local events, lend a helping hand, or simply meet new faces, NextDoor Circle is the perfect space to foster relationships and strengthen the bonds that make our community special. Together, we can create a more connected, supportive, and vibrant neighborhood—one circle at a time.

We host yearly dodgeball tournaments where neighbors team up for a day of fun, friendly competition, and high-energy action! Whether you're dodging, diving, or just cheering from the sidelines, it’s the perfect way to connect with your community and enjoy some local excitement.

Potlucks are weekly every Friday bring your favorite dish and enjoy an afternoon of great food, laughter, and community as we come together to celebrate the flavors of our neighborhood.

We can’t wait to meet you and welcome you into our circle of neighbors, friends, and changemakers.

  • Potlucks every Friday starting at 5pm
  • Community Clean-ups
  • Game nights and more !!!
